C — Both: overall + per-category

A master total cap plus individual category limits. Most powerful but takes more effort to set up. Best for serious budgeters.

As a data analyst perspective: Option B (per-category) gives the richest insight and is how most people discover their actual problem areas. Option A is easiest to start with. Option C is ideal long-term but needs more setup.
